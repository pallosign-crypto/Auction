Legal Framework & Foreign Investment

Understanding Tanzanian property laws and foreign ownership regulations.

Property Ownership

All land in Tanzania is publicly owned and vested in the President as trustee for citizens. Foreigners can acquire long-term leaseholds through derivative rights.

  • • 33, 66, or 99-year lease terms available
  • • Foreign ownership limit: 70% in apartment buildings
  • • TIC approval required for large investments

Investment Process

Our streamlined process ensures compliance with all legal requirements while maximizing investment potential.

  • • Due diligence and title verification
  • • Legal documentation preparation
  • • Registration and transfer facilitation

Tax & Fees

Transparent fee structure with no hidden costs. All statutory charges clearly outlined.
